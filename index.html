Welcome to  Sri Athmananda Trust®

Athmananda Tust is an organization for Human Service brought into existence from the inspiration of Sri Sadguru Athmananda Swamy (1885-1940) a holy soul who attained great knowledge early in life and lived to enable Human community to higher consciousness and wisdom, Swamy’s life history is the depiction for it.


Sri Athmananda Swamy set a perfect example for us to follow scriptures and the Trust runs on the same principles. Since the incorporation the Trust is working towards realizing the vision foreseen by Sri Athmananda Swamy.


We hope, on this website you could know well about Sri Sadguru Athmananda Swamy and be one among several who got inspired by the way Swamy lived and served the human community.


Great souls who are aspiring to join the team and live in the guidelines of Swamy Athmananda are requested to contact the committee.
