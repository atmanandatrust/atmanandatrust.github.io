Sri Sadguru Athmananda Swamiji's Divya Charitre

Sri Sri Athmananda Swamy

Sri Sri Sri Sadguru Athmanada Swamy is a holy soul (Paramathma) was born in Srirangam the holy city of Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple.

On 28-04-1885 (as per Hindu Calender Vaishaka Shuklapaksha Poornima, the birth day of God Kumaraswamy & Bhagavan Buddha) His name was Sri Doraiswamy, Father's name was Sri Veeraswamy Pillai and mother name was Srimati Manonmani Ammal.


His parents were religious and followers of Sri Vaishnava Sampradaya . Young Sri Athamanand was prone to experience of spiritual reverie His early spiritual experience included going into a state of rapture while watching the idols and photos of gods. In his childhood Sri Athmananda had no interest in material world. Because of these haven charcter, Sri Doraiswamy became Sri Sri Sri Sadguru Athmanaanda Swamy.


(Chronology of main Events related to Sri Sri Sadguru Swami Athmananda)

Previous Life

Name: Sri Chinmayananda Desikalu

Place: Holy Town of Goddess Meenakshi at Madurai in Tamilnadu lived a celibate life and showed the spiritual path to his disciples


(Life Events between 28.04.1885 to 16.04.1940)


Place of Birth: Srirangam the Temple City of Lord Ranganatha swamy Temple at Tamil Nadu


Date of Birth: 28-04-1885(Vaishaka Poornima - God Kumarswamy and Bhagavan Buddha Jayanti)


Birth Name: Sri Doraiswamy


Father's Name: Sri Veeraswamy Pillai


Mother's Name: Srimati Manonmani Ammal


Education: Started with the age of 5 years 5 months and 5 days (Mastery in 14 language including Samskrit)


Guru's Name: Srimad Arulmarri Ekanga Swamiji (Sri Vaishnava Bhodacharya at one of the Sri Vaishnava Mutt at Srirangam)


Bestowed Name: Because of Mastery in Sri Vaishnava Philosophy Guru Arulmarri Ekanga Swamiji bestowed new name " SRI ATHMANANDA SWAMY" Year 1901. After the Samadi of Srimad Arulmarri Ekanga Swamy ,become the Head of Sri Vaishnava Mutt at Srirangam(1905).


Moved to Bangalore: After 4 years’ experience in Mutt as a Supreme Head of Sri Vaishnava Mutt he came out from the mutt as he was not comfortable with activities in the mutt.


Job at Military: As per his Father's wish he moved to Bangalore along with his father and started his work at Military (1906)


Moved to Hubli to Visit: After 2 years’ Service in Military and resignation he met Sadguru Sri Siddarooda Swamy at Hubli and received holy Sadguru Sri Sri Siddarooda Swamyji Nayana Deeksha & Shiva Pachakshari from Sadguru Sidharooda Swamyji Bhramhashri Ekananda Swamy alias as per advise from Sadguru Siddarooda Swamiji he visited to Bramhashri Ekananda Swamy alias Bhramshri Sri Kambali Swamji at Bangalore Sri Kambali Swamy for further knowledge about Brahmajnana Year 1914 (aged 29 years)


Moved to Shimoga Forest: For deep knowledge about Soul and Bhrahma he moved to thick forest at Shimoga for Meditation and after 2&1\2 years he came back to Bangalore along with Lancer Team who were found him in forest.


Seva to Society as a Vaidya: After his enlightment he served to Society as a Vaidya for Physical body and Guru for Mukti


Date of Mahasamadi: After his 55 years’ service to mankind he attained Maha Samadi on 16-04-1940 (Sri Ramanavami - Chaitra Shukla Navami)
