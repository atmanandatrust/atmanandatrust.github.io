Sri Athmananda Trust

The Athmananda Trust was first Registered on 15 October 2003 during the period of Srimati Navaneetamma Devi (1926-2004) then it was reconstituted on 23rd  March 2015 with aims of serving the Mankind.

7)    Sri V Ankappa  Reddy
